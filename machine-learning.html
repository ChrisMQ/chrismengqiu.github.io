About this work

I am interested in how ideas from machine learning — including deep learning and causal inference — can be adapted to the questions and data that arise in the social and behavioral sciences.

A current direction explores generative AI for Monte Carlo simulation studies, aiming to make methodological research faster and more reproducible.
