Research Group

The Infinite Modeling Lab

We are a research group at the University of California, Merced, studying flexible models for learning and inference under uncertainty. Our work draws on Bayesian nonparametrics, variational inference, and deep learning, and spans structural equation modeling, missing data, and causal inference — with the goal of building methods that support reliable inference from real-world data.

Overview

About the lab

The Infinite Modeling Lab (IML) takes its name from a double meaning. "Infinite" points to the infinite-dimensional models at the heart of our work in Bayesian nonparametrics — and to our conviction that good research isn't confined to any single modeling framework. We follow the problem, not the method, drawing on whatever combination of tools it takes to get reliable answers from real data.
